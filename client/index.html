Features

Contact Management

Store, search, and organize every lead and customer. Add notes, tag by stage, and never lose a conversation again.

Deal Pipeline

Visualize your sales funnel with a Kanban board. Move deals from Lead to Closed Won with a single click.

Task Tracker

Never miss a follow-up. Create tasks tied to deals or contacts, set due dates, one click to mark complete.
